# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

adaptive

## Users

Atrium mobile serves AI-native technical and product teams who need to stay meaningfully involved with human and agent work away from a desk. They review progress, answer questions, steer sessions, communicate, inspect artifacts, and handle time-sensitive decisions in short or interrupted contexts.

## Product Purpose

The native apps keep Atrium's collaboration loop available without turning mobile into a reduced notification client. Success means users can understand current work and take the right next action confidently on either iOS or Android, while deeper desktop-scale work remains easy to locate and resume.

## Positioning

Work with agents together, wherever work needs attention.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable, alive. Mobile should preserve Atrium's precise and responsive character while adapting navigation, controls, typography, motion, and system integration to each operating system. The desired emotional outcome is amplification without anxiety: important work is present and actionable, not constantly demanding attention.

Linear and Slack remain interaction references for precision and conversational legibility, while Apple Human Interface Guidelines and Material Design 3 govern native structure and behavior.

## Anti-references

Do not ship a generic AI dashboard compressed onto a phone, a web interface disguised as native, or a chat app with agents bolted on. Avoid decorative gradients, interchangeable card stacks, ornamental glass, vague agent-magic language, custom replacements for standard platform controls, and desktop density squeezed into compact layouts.

## Design Principles

1. **Keep the next intervention obvious.** Short mobile visits should quickly reveal what needs attention and what can wait.
2. **Preserve the collaboration thread.** Conversation, agent state, decisions, and resulting work should remain connected even when presented progressively.
3. **Adapt rather than imitate.** Maintain conceptual parity with web while honoring iOS and Android navigation, back behavior, typography, insets, controls, and accessibility.
4. **Amplify without urgency theater.** Presence and live state should feel useful and alive, not noisy or manipulative.
5. **Make recovery first-class.** Offline, reconnecting, partial, failed, and deferred states must remain comprehensible and actionable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet or exceed WCAG 2.2 AA where applicable and honor native assistive technologies. Support Dynamic Type or platform font scaling, VoiceOver and TalkBack semantics, Reduce Motion or removed animations, increased contrast, 44 pt iOS and 48 dp Android targets, safe-area and system-bar insets, system Back behavior, and non-color-only status communication.
